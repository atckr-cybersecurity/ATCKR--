WHAT IS ATCKR?

ATCKR is our cybersecurity simulation platform designed to teach kids security concepts through interactive gaming. Currently, we are in the process of developing brand new 8-bit games which will showcase features that will engage excitement within our target audience!

HOW DID IT COME TO BE?

We developed this platform with one priority in mind: helping elementary and middle school students understand fundamentals of learning in different ways. We acknowledge beneficial aspects about lecturing, but also know that gaming can be supplementary to learning. Our program currently specializes in a 3-level game which instructs them on topics such as phishing, malware protection and enhanced vocabulary. Our goal? To see the projection of development in not only kid's learning, but to also acknowledge interactive foundations can aid in supplementing lecturing as well.
